From Dark

Creators - Michael Torres, Edwin Torres
October 2020 - December 2020

Our take on a 2D Dungeon crawler. Explore rooms and search through barrels blocking your way. Fight enemies in a unique battle minigame based on crane game mechanics. Don't linger in a room too long or you will be engulfed by the ever seeking darkness.


Edwin - Major responsibility for dungeon room generation and movement mechanics

Michael - Major responsibility for battle minigame and enemy generation

Created for a class project within a couple months in PICO-8 (a Lua based virtual console).
